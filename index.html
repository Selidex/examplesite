School

School has always been one of my refuges. It is one of the places where I felt seen. I went to Holliday ISD, in Holliday, Texas. I was in the marching band, going to State all 3 years that the competition was held. I was also a member of the One Act Play as the soundboard technician, proceeding to Regionals for two years. I also was a member of the Speech and Debate team, going to State for Lincoln Doulgass debate my senior year. I graduated 4th of the class of 2012. Afterwards, I attended Midwestern State University for 4 years, Majoring first in Psychology, and then in Computer Science with a Psychology minor. Unfortunately, I had to take a leave of absence due to health issues. After my recovery, I continued my education at Holberton Tulsa, where I spent two years studying full stack development with a specialization in AR/VR development.

Teaching

As of the time of this writing, this is my first full year of teaching professionally. I have taken on several student teaching roles, both as study leader in high school, as well as tutoring while in college. I also was the Web Development instructor for one of Urban Coders Guild's summer camps in 2023. Teaching has been a passion of mine since I was a little kid, and it is a dream come true to be teaching web development to young minds today.

  • Digmon World

  • Final Fantasy VII
